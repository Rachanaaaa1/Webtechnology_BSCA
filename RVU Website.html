  • ABOUT RVU

    • RV Educational Institutions (RVEI), governed by Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST), is recognised among the few value-based and quality-oriented educational groups in the country. The Trust endeavours to impart quality education to all strata of society. RV University is a State Private University which has been established in Karnataka State with RSST as the sponsoring body through Act No.11 of 2019, passed by the Karnataka Legislature. The Missions of RSST and RV University is “Excellence in Education with Societal Commitment”.
